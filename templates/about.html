About KTU Student Concern Portal

The Koforidua Technical University Student Concern Portal is designed to facilitate communication between students and university administration. It allows students to submit issues or concerns, and enables admins and subadmins to manage and respond to these issues efficiently.

This portal aims to improve transparency, responsiveness, and overall student satisfaction by providing a streamlined platform for issue tracking and resolution.
